flour,main,dressing
2,2,4
1.Take the * flour in a deep bowl, mix well and knead to make a semi-stiff smooth using enough water. Cover and keep aside for at least 10 to 15 minutes.
2.Divide the dough into 4 equal portions and keep aside.
3.Prepare the stuffing using * combined with *. Divide the stuffing into 4 equal portions and keep aside.
4.Roll out a portion of the dough into a 100 mm. (4”) diameter circle using a little wheat flour.
5.Place a portion of the stuffing in the centre, fold the edges towards the centre and seal well so the stuffing doesn’t spill out.
6.Roll out again into 125 mm. (5”) diameter circle using a little wheat flour.
7.Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) and cook each roti, using a little oil, till golden brown spots appear on both the sides.
8.Repeat steps 2 to 5 to make 3 more rotis. Serve immediately.

flour,main
2,2
1.Combine yeast, sugar and 5 tbsp of lukewarm water in a bowl, mix gently and cover with a lid and keep aside for 4 to 5 minutes.
2.Combine the * flour and all other ingredients * with salt and the above yeast-sugar paste in a deep bowl and knead into a soft dough using little water.
3.Cover the dough with a lid or wet muslin cloth and allow it prove till it increases in volume slightly (approx. 30 minutes).
4.Divide the dough into 6 equal portions.
5.Heat a non-stick tava (griddle) and cook each naan, using a little oil, till golden brown spots appear on both the sides.
6.Repeat steps 2 to 5 to make 3 more naans. Serve immediately.
7.Brush each naan with a little butter and serve immediately.
